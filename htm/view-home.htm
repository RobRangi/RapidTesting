Our Services

Achieve Product Quality through testing

Rapid Testing is a company dedicated to helping companies to get more out of their testing services. We align with the Context Driven approach to testing and with this in mind we provide the following services to enable this:-

  • Providing testing courses to mentor and up-skill your testers to be critical thinkers, better problem solvers and to think outside the square and to add greater value. Too many companies see testers as "button pushers", however by looking at testing as more than proving specifications, we can help testers provide information that managers need (whether it is good or bad).
  • Provision of test consultancy services to evaluate your testing approach, strategies and teams
  • Provision of testers for engagements that are more than bodies for projects - they provide value
  • Provision of test coaching, mentoring and development for small companies with limited numbers of testers to help build excellent test teams
