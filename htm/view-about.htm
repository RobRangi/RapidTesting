about us

Brian Osman
Principal Consultant
We deliver training that is based on improving the way testers see, think, interact with others and with the system under test. This is one way to show our value-add as testers. We are community focused liaising with like-minded, free thinking testers and take the opportunity to present at conferences and SIGsT groups.

Brian founded the Kiwi Workshop on Software Testing (KWST), an invite-only peer conference with 17 New Zealand test leaders facilitated with help and guidance from James Bach.
